Top Government Scholarships

Mukhyamantri Yuva Swavalamban Yojana


The Mukhyamantri Yuva Swavalamban Yojana (MYSY) scholarship is a government scheme in Gujarat that provides financial assistance to students from economically disadvantaged families.

Gyan Sadhana Scholarship Scheme


The Gyan Sadhana Scholarship by the Gujarat government supports meritorious students from low-income families with financial aid for Classes 9-12.

Vice Chancellor Swayam Scholarship


The Vice Chancellor Swayam Scholarship supports economically challenged B.Tech students by covering tuition, hostel, and mess fees.

Digital Gujarat Scholarship


The Digital Gujarat Scholarship is a government initiative providing financial aid to students from economically weaker sections in Gujarat.

Chief Minister Scholarship Scheme


The Chief Minister Scholarship Scheme (CMSS) provides financial assistance to economically weaker students for higher education.
